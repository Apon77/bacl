    Brigadier General AKM Mahfuzul Haque (Retd)

    BACL - Managing Director

    AKM Mahfuzul Haque served in the Bangladesh Army from January 1978 to January 2012 and retired as a Brigadier General. As the Managing Director of BACL for the past six years, he has gathered vast experience in the management of construction and execution of projects in different sectors, including the Bangladesh Armed Forces. He has multi-dimensioned networking and associations in various sectors. Brigadier General Mahfuz has established the company, ensuring adherence to the rules and regulations, quality, and timely operation.

    He is a result-driven, motivated and resourceful managing director with a proven ability to develop and strengthen his teams to execute and implement target-oriented projects efficiently. He has excellent communication skills and can establish sustainable and rational relationships with customers, suppliers, and other stakeholders within and outside Bangladesh. He was elected President of Mirpur DOHS Porishod for three consecutive terms and dedicated himself to improving the lives and livelihood of the residents of Mirpur DOHS.

    He is married with two children and two grandchildren.

Broad Arrow Constructoin Limited (BACL)

Broad Arrow Constructoin Limited (BACL) is an independent engineering construction company with an objective to provide complete and international standard technical approach in the construction industry of Bangladesh. New Allahar Dan Mayer Dua Enterprise ventured into the construction area as early as 2012 and Broad Arrow Constructoin Limited (BACL) was formed in 2017 to join the venture. The company was created out of a vision of Lt Col Md Aminul Islam (Retd.), the CEO & Chairman of Broad Arrow Construction Limited, to serve the nation with top-quality professionals.


BACL consists of professionals, experts in the development sectors, and experienced management. The organization has launched country-wide nation-building activities by completing several multi-storied buildings, hydraulic structures, deep tube wells, water purification plants, irrigation, flood control projects, etc. BACL is also experienced in constructing piling (situ/bored/caisson), pile cap & retaining structures,etc.


BACL is also experienced in supply of different construction materials i.e. Sylhet Sand, Filling Sand, Stone Chips etc.
